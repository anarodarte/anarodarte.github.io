About Me

I am a curious and motivated UX designer who is excited to create inclusive designs, champions for an effortless user experience in every field of work, thrives in a team environment and strives to be a role model for other designers who do not come from traditional tech backgrounds.

Background

Research Enthusiast

I started my career in the healthcare field. After a being on the frontlines of COVID and working in labs, I realized I gained a plethora of experience in research methods and problem solving. I began searching for ways I could apply these methods in other fields.

Natural Art Lover

Over time, I realized I had given up all my hobbies and interests to pursue my degree in healthcare. During COVID, I began exploring my creative side using traditional physical and modern digital mediums. This opened up my eyes to the possibility of combining my passion for art/design and research methods.

Pursuing UX/UI Design

In early 2020, I decided to start teaching myself UX/UI Design. I started off with Udemy courses, went on to complete the Google Certification in Foundations of UX Design, and finished a UX/UI Design bootcamp with the University of California Irvine.
